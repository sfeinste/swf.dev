Satchel of Storing

A simple app for keeping track of shared party loot in D&D. No more arguing about who's carrying the healing potions or where you left that mysterious key from three sessions ago.

I built this after one too many sessions where we lost track of important items or forgot crucial plot details. It handles the bookkeeping so you can focus on the adventure. Track shared inventory, campaign notes, NPCs you've met, and organizations you're dealing with.

The inventory tracker knows about item weights and bag capacity, so you'll know when your party's pack mule is overloaded. The notes section is searchable, making it easy to find that NPC's name you forgot or recall which town had the suspicious mayor.

Works for any tabletop RPG, not just D&D. Built with React Native and uses cloud sync so the whole party can stay updated.

Available now on the App Store. Free to use for your campaign.
